Idea Challenge

idea-challenge is the I-Wish Discover workflow for stress-testing an idea before PRD and execution planning.

It is the canonical replacement surface for upstream PRFAQ / Working Backwards in the I-Wish system.

Best Uses

  • New product or solution ideas
  • Concepts that feel fuzzy or too assumption-heavy
  • Pre-PRD concept forging
  • Major plan-level pivots

How It Works

  • Clarify user, problem, stakes, and solution
  • Challenge the promise and objections
  • Attach socratic-review for questioning
  • Attach unique-advantage-evaluator for strategy and business differentiation
  • Attach research when claims need evidence

Outputs

  • idea-challenge-{project}.md
  • idea-challenge-{project}-distillate.md
  • Optional biz-stack.md

# Unique Advantage Evaluator Integration Guide

## What It Is

`unique-advantage-evaluator` is I-Wish's strategic business-advantage lens.

It helps the system evaluate whether an idea, plan, or project has meaningful differentiation and durable leverage, and also helps propose stronger business options.

## Two Modes

### Stress-Test

Use for:

- `idea-challenge`
- PRD / product brief challenge
- `pivot-project`

### Solution-Proposal

Use for:

- `research`
- planning conversations
- shaping a `biz-stack`

## Related Capabilities

- `idea-challenge`
- `research`
- `socratic-review`
- `pivot-project`

Unique Advantage Evaluator

unique-advantage-evaluator is the I-Wish strategy add-on for testing and strengthening business advantage.

It is the canonical rename for the earlier strategy-moat-evaluator concept.

Two Modes

  • Stress-Test: challenge differentiation, copyability, defensibility, and business-model weakness
  • Solution-Proposal: propose stronger pricing, distribution, wedge, retention, and biz-stack options

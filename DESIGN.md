# Design System Tokens

## Colors
*   **Primary Green:** `#00DF9A` (Active buttons, highlights)
*   **Secondary Green:** `#059669` (Success indicators, safe states)
*   **Background Dark:** `#0F172A` (Low-fatigue dark background)
*   **Text Light:** `#F8FAFC` (High contrast text)
*   **Gray Border:** `#334155` (Subtle borders)

## Typography
*   **Family:** `Outfit, sans-serif`
*   **Heading Weight:** `700`
*   **Body Weight:** `400`

# Step IC-03: Customer FAQ

## Goal

Stress-test the concept from the outside in.

## Cover Questions Like

- why switch from current behavior?
- what friction blocks adoption?
- what trust objections exist?
- what practical objections exist?
- what edge case would a skeptical user raise?

## Standard

- questions must be hard, not softball CTA questions
- answers must be specific and believable
- if an answer is weak, name the gap directly
- if the concept is non-commercial, replace pricing questions with adoption/effort/sustainability questions where appropriate

## Artifact Update

Append the Customer FAQ section to `idea-challenge-{project}.md`.
Append a `<!-- coaching-notes-stage-3 -->` block for customer objections, adoption risks, and scope signals.

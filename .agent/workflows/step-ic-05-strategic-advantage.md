# Step IC-05: Strategic Advantage

## Goal

Move beyond “interesting product idea” into “potentially strong business direction”.

## Required Capability

Attach `unique-advantage-evaluator` when:

- the concept needs differentiation
- pricing, distribution, defensibility, or retention logic is unclear
- the user asks about business advantage or competitive edge

## Evaluate in Two Directions

### Stress-Test

- Is the advantage real?
- Is it copyable?
- Is this just operational effectiveness?
- Does the business model reinforce or weaken the idea?

### Solution-Proposal

- What stronger pricing model exists?
- What stronger distribution wedge exists?
- What retention or lock-in mechanism exists?
- What should the `biz-stack` look like?

## Artifact Update

- add strategic advantage section to `idea-challenge-{project}.md`
- create `biz-stack.md` when needed
- append a `<!-- coaching-notes-stage-5 -->` block for rejected strategic paths and surviving advantage hypotheses

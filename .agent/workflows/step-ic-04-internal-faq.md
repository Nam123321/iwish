# Step IC-04: Internal FAQ

## Goal

Stress-test the concept from the builder and stakeholder side.

## Cover Questions Like

- hardest technical problem
- resource and timeline realism
- what kills the project
- what is strategically weak
- what trade-off is being ignored
- what the team is pretending is easy

## Add-On Rules

- use `socratic-review` to challenge assumptions
- pull in `research` if feasibility or market claims are uncertain
- adapt the internal pressure to concept type instead of blindly assuming a venture-backed startup frame

## Artifact Update

Append the Internal FAQ section to `idea-challenge-{project}.md`.
Append a `<!-- coaching-notes-stage-4 -->` block for execution risks, dependencies, timeline realism, and unknowns.
